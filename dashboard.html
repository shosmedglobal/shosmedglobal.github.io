Entrance Exam Overview by Faculty

Charles University has five medical faculties. Each sets its own entrance exam. Here's what to expect at each one.

LF3 — Third Faculty of Medicine Prague SHOS Partner

Subjects Biology (30 MCQ) + Chemistry (30 MCQ) = 60 questions
Format Electronic written test (on your own laptop) + 10-min oral interview
Exam Dates May 29, 2026 (primary) · July 2, 2026 (alternative)
International Exams Israel (Jan 4, May 1, Jul 5) · UK (Apr 19, Jul 2) + more via agencies
Exam-Free Entry Yes — up to 25 students via IB (HL 19+ in Chem/Bio/English), A-Levels (AAB), or SAT (85th %ile)
Admitted ~90 students per year

LF1 — First Faculty of Medicine Prague

Subjects Biology (25 Q) + Chemistry (25 Q) + General Science (25 Q) = 75 MCQ
Format Round 1: Written MCQ (90 min) · Round 2: MMI — 5 stations, 10 min each (online)
Exam Dates Jun 8, 2026 (written) · Jun 25, 2026 (MMI)
Exam-Free Entry No — written exam is mandatory for all applicants

LF2 — Second Faculty of Medicine Prague

Subjects Biology (20 Q) + Chemistry (20 Q) + Physics (20 Q) + Logic (15 Q) = 75 questions
Format Written MCQ (90 min) + Interview for top scorers
Exam Dates May 27, 2026 (regular) · Jul 15, 2026 (alternative)
International Exams Tel-Aviv, Dubai, Frankfurt, London, Seoul, Toronto + more
Exam-Free Entry Yes — IB 35+ (HL 19+), A-Levels AAA, AP 85th %ile, MCAT 512+, BSc GPA 3.5+

LFHK — Faculty of Medicine, Hradec Kralove Hradec

Subjects Biology (25 pts) + Chemistry (25 pts) + Physics or Math (10 pts) = 60 questions
Format Online video-proctored MCQ (120 min) + MS Teams interview
Exam Dates May 13 & Jun 3, 2026 (online) · 105 places available
Exam-Free Entry Yes — A-levels AAB in Chemistry, Biology, Maths/Physics

LFPL — Faculty of Medicine, Pilsen Pilsen

Subjects Biology (25 Q) + Chemistry (25 Q) + Physics (25 Q) = 75 MCQ
Format Written MCQ, 1 point per correct answer, no penalty
Exam Date Jun 3, 2026 (Pilsen) + agency terms May-Aug in Israel, Germany, UAE, Greece

Required Documents

  • Completed online application via is.cuni.cz
  • High school diploma (certified copy, translated if not in English/Czech)
  • Official transcript of grades
  • Valid passport copy
  • Application fee payment confirmation
  • Nostrification of diploma (Czech recognition of foreign education) — can be done after admission
  • Health certificate (some faculties require this at enrollment)
  • Proof of health insurance (required for student visa)

Third Faculty of Medicine (LF3)

Charles University · Prague 10, Vinohrady

Founded as part of Charles University (est. 1348), LF3 is a modern medical faculty offering a 6-year General Medicine program fully in English. Graduates earn the MUDr. degree (equivalent to MD), recognized across the EU and eligible for USMLE and US residency.

Why LF3?

🎯
Only Biology + Chemistry

LF3 is the only Prague faculty that tests just 2 subjects — no physics, no logic section. Focused prep, better results.

🌍
EU-Recognized Degree

Your MUDr. degree lets you practice medicine anywhere in the European Union — plus you're ECFMG-eligible for the US.

🏥
Top Clinical Training

Primary hospital: University Hospital Kralovske Vinohrady. Plus rotations at Hospital Na Bulovce, Thomayer Hospital, IKEM, and more.

💰
Affordable Tuition

~$111,000 for the entire 6-year degree. That's less than 2 years at most US medical schools.

📋
Exam-Free Admission Available

Up to 25 students admitted without the entrance exam via IB, A-Levels, or SAT scores.

🇺🇸
US Dept. of Education Listed

LF3 is specifically accredited for the study of US students — your degree is recognized for federal loan eligibility and ECFMG certification.
