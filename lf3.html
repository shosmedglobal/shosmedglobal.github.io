Why LF3 Specifically

What Makes This Faculty Different

Charles University has three medical faculties. LF3 is the one that combines modern clinical exposure with strong international reach.

English-taught since 1991

Over three decades of experience running a full MD program in English. You are not a beta student.

Clinical exposure from Year 1

Classes are taught inside Kralovske Vinohrady University Hospital. You see real patients and wards early, not just lecture halls.

EU-recognized MD degree

Your diploma is automatically recognized across all 27 EU member states under mutual recognition directives. No additional equivalency exams.

ECFMG-certified for US residency

Graduates are eligible to sit the USMLE and enter the US residency match. Our own co-founders matched into competitive US programs as LF3 graduates.

Intimate cohort size

Small English-track class allows close faculty contact, easier access to research, and a real alumni network, not a lecture-hall-of-500 experience.

Practice in Europe, the US, or both

Your MD opens doors across the EU without extra credentialing. Combine with USMLE and you can practice in the US too. Very few paths keep both options open.

Campus

Kralovske Vinohrady University Hospital

Prague, Czech Republic
Fakultni Nemocnice
Kralovske Vinohrady
University Hospital · Vinohrady District
See on Google Maps →

LF3 is unusual among European medical faculties in being located directly inside a large teaching hospital. You learn anatomy, pathology, and clinical medicine in the same building where patients are being treated. By the end of Year 2, most students are already rotating through wards. This integrated-hospital model is the single most common reason graduates say they felt ready for real clinical work by the time they reached Year 5 and 6.

The hospital is in Prague's Vinohrady district: central, walkable, well-connected by metro and trams. Student housing, grocery stores, cafes, and the main university buildings are all within 10 to 20 minutes.
