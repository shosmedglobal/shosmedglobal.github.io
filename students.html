The Match Pathway

From European Med School to US Residency

Six steps every IMG walks. We've walked all six, and help you plan yours.

Step 1 · Plan

Map your 4-year timeline

We convert your European med school calendar into a Step 1 / Step 2 / electives / ERAS plan that actually fits. Not a generic US-MD timeline repackaged.

Step 2 · USMLE Step 1

Pass Step 1 with confidence

Step 1 is pass/fail, but it can still tank your cycle. High-yield 1-on-1 sessions from physicians who scored competitively on every Step.

Step 3 · USMLE Step 2 CK

Score competitively on the one that matters

Step 2 CK is the score residency programs read first. We build a study plan around where you're losing points, not around a generic schedule.

Step 4 · US Clinical Experience

Land US clinical rotations, with a letter to show for it

Observerships, externships, and hands-on US letters of recommendation. Where to look, how to ask, and how to turn a 2-week rotation into a strong LoR.

Step 5 · ERAS Application

Submit an application that gets interviews

Program list, CV, and personal statement reviewed by physicians who now sit on residency interview panels, not by a consultant who read a book.

Step 6 · Match Day

Interview, rank, match

Mock interviews, rank order strategy, and match-week support from people who've been through it. Twice.

Both of our founders completed this exact pathway: from Charles University in Prague to US residencies, and now to subspecialty fellowships at major academic medical centers.
