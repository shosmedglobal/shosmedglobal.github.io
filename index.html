Who We Are

Built by a Physician Who Walked This Path

SHOS Med Global was founded by Dr. Eli Zolotov, a Hematology & Oncology Fellow at Hackensack University Medical Center, who graduated from the Third Faculty of Medicine at Charles University in Prague.

Having navigated the entire journey - from European medical school to US residency to subspecialty fellowship - Dr. Zolotov understands the challenges international medical graduates face. SHOS Med Global was born from years of mentoring over 100 students through this exact pathway.

Our mission is twofold: connect ambitious pre-med students with outstanding European medical education, and mentor international medical graduates toward successful US residency placement.
