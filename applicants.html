Annual Tuition
~$18,500*

About $18,500 per year for tuition at Charles University LF3, with installment plans available. A fraction of US medical school annual tuition, and you skip the four undergraduate years of tuition that normally precede US medical school.

Time to MD
6 years

Direct from high school to MD. No pre-med bachelor's required. The standard US path takes 8 years total (4 undergrad + 4 medical school).

Entrance Exam

30 Biology + 30 Chemistry, 2 hours, online, no negative marking. No MCAT. No pre-med GPA race. Our QBank is aligned exactly to the tested topics.

A short oral interview follows the written exam. Interview preparation is included with our Full Application Package.
