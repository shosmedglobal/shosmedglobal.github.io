Exam-Free Admission Pathway

Up to 25 students per year may be admitted without the entrance exam, based on outstanding academic results:

  • A-Levels: Minimum A, A, B in English, Chemistry, and Biology (2024/2025 or later)
  • IB Higher Level: Minimum 19 points across Chemistry, Biology, and English (2024/2025 or later)
  • SAT or comparable: Minimum 85th percentile in Biology, Chemistry, and English (2024/2025 or later)
  • German secondary school: Average grade better than 1.30 (2024/2025 graduation)
  • Bachelor's degree: In natural or health sciences, graduated 2020/2021 or later
After You're Admitted
  • Tuition: 420,000 CZK/year (~$18,500 USD). Bank transfer only. Installment plans available.
  • Program: 6-year General Medicine, MUDr. degree (equivalent to MD). English-taught since 1991. US-accredited.
  • Language: Czech proficiency required by end of year 2 for clinical rotations.
  • Health insurance: EU students: EHIC + Czech health fund (VZP/ZPMV). Non-EU: private insurance or VZP.
  • Visa: Start early (by June). Admission letter provided for your application.
  • Documents: Verified high school certificate (with apostille), transcript, passport copy.
  • Nostrification: Certificate recognition through the university (CZK 990) or Czech Ministry.
  • Vaccinations: Hepatitis B and measles documentation required.
  • Enrollment: September, latest October 20.
