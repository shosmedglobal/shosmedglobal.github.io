For Parents

Your Child's Medical Career, Made Clear

We know this is a big decision. Here are the facts parents ask about most.

Is Prague safe?

Prague is ranked among the safest cities in Europe, with a lower crime rate than most major US cities. Students thrive in its walkable, vibrant environment.

Will my child's degree be recognized in the US?

Yes. Charles University is fully recognized by ECFMG and meets WFME accreditation standards. Graduates are eligible to apply for US residency programs.

What about student loans?

US Federal Student Loans may be available depending on the faculty. Total costs are significantly lower than US or Caribbean medical schools even without loans.

How do graduates match into US residencies?

Through the same USMLE and ERAS process as any medical graduate. Our mentorship program provides step-by-step guidance through the entire process.
