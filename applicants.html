Why Czech Republic Over Caribbean?

Skip 4 Years of College

Caribbean and US med schools require a bachelor's degree first - that's 4 extra years and $100K-$200K before you even start med school.

EU-Recognized Degree

After graduating from LF3 at Charles University, you can also practice medicine in Europe if you choose — giving you career flexibility across two continents.

Fraction of the Cost

When you add college tuition to Caribbean med school, you're looking at $200K-$350K total. Charles University: ~$111K for the entire degree.

Live in Europe

Study in Prague — one of Europe's most vibrant, safe, and affordable capital cities, with world-class hospitals and clinical training sites throughout the city.

Globally Recognized

Charles University is a top-ranked, centuries-old institution - not a for-profit school with uncertain accreditation.

No MCAT Required

Skip the MCAT entirely. Charles University has its own entrance exam focused on biology and chemistry — no standardized US testing needed.
