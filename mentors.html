Co-founder

Eli Zolotov, MD

Adult Hematology/Oncology
Adult Hematology/Oncology Internal Medicine
Charles University, Prague - Third Faculty of Medicine
Internal Medicine Residency - Hackensack University Medical Center
Hematology/Oncology Fellowship - HUMC John Theurer Cancer Center

Board-certified in Internal Medicine. One of few Charles University graduates to reach a competitive US subspecialty fellowship in Hematology/Oncology at one of the nation's top cancer centers. 30+ peer-reviewed publications in leading hematology journals including Blood, European Journal of Haematology, and the American Journal of Hematology. Co-investigator on multi-center clinical trials with Mayo Clinic, Stanford, and MD Anderson. Awarded the 2025 ASH Abstract Achievement Award by the American Society of Hematology. Actively researching novel therapies in multiple myeloma and CAR T-cell therapy, with presentations at ASH and ASCO.

Co-founder

Anat Sigal, MD

Pediatric Gastroenterology
Gastroenterology Pediatrics
Charles University, Prague - Third Faculty of Medicine
Pediatrics Residency - Hackensack University Medical Center
Pediatric Gastroenterology Fellowship - NYU Langone (Starting July 2026)

Matched into Pediatrics as an IMG and secured a competitive Pediatric Gastroenterology fellowship at NYU Langone, one of the most sought-after pediatric subspecialties. With 70+ citations across her publications, including research in the Journal of Adolescent Health, SLEEP Advances (Oxford Academic), and Pediatrics (AAP), Anat has presented at NASPGHAN (the leading pediatric GI conference) and the AAP National Conference, and is a researcher in the NIH ECHO Program (Environmental Influences on Child Health Outcomes), a major multi-center initiative studying how environmental factors affect child health.
